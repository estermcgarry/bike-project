About

Baile Bikes making journeys easier and enjoyable!

Baile Bike is a self-service bike rental system that operates alongside existing public transport options in Dublin. It represents an alternative to commuting between home and work. It also enables you to get out and about to enjoy Dublin city at your leisure. Using Baile Bike you can avoid the heavy traffic and contribute to reducing the pollution in the atmosphere.

  • Baile Bike is open to everyone from 14 years of age.
  • The first 30 minutes of use is free on every bike
  • Stations Open: 05:00 am - 00:30 am
  • Return a bike : 24 hours
